User Guide

You can control the drone using buttons and keyboard shortcuts.

Buttons:

Place (Enter):

Click the "Place" button to place the drone on the grid. It will be placed at the top of the grid, facing north.

Move (Arrow Up/W):

Click the "Move" button to move the drone one cell in the direction it's facing.

Left (ArrowLeft/A):

Click the "Left" button to rotate the drone 90 degrees to the left.

Right (Arrow Right/D):

Click the "Right" button to rotate the drone 90 degrees to the right.

Report:

Click the "Report" button to get a report of the drone's current position and direction.

Attack (Spacebar):

Click the "Attack" button to simulate an attack action. The drone will attack in the direction it's facing, and an explosion may occur.
